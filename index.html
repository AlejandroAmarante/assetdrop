About

Our Mission

As a fellow gamedev, I know how hard it can be to find good free models that are actually usable. That's why we're volunteering to gather cool, game-ready 3D assets from talented artists all over, making it easier for creators like us to find what we need without the usual hassle.

Why Quality Matters

We try to pick assets that are well-made with clean topology, optimized for games, and nicely textured. We focus on sharing assets that are reliable and ready to use in your projects.

FAQ

How do I download the 3D assets?
You can't download assets directly from our site. We provide previews and links to the original Sketchfab pages where you can download the assets according to the creator's licensing terms.
Are all assets free to use?
All assets are hand picked, and are at the very least CC0 Attribution. If you encounter an asset that is not at least CC0 Attribution, please utilize the contact form with the subject "Report Asset".
How often do you add new assets?
We regularly update our collection with new assets. New additions are typically made weekly, featuring the latest high-quality assets from the Sketchfab community.
Can I submit my own assets to be featured?
Yes! If you're a 3D artist with high-quality game assets on Sketchfab, you can contact us through our contact form. We review submissions based on quality, originality, and usefulness for game development.
